Chris Vazquez

Hi! I am a second-year political science Ph.D. student at Massachussetts Institute of Technology focused on American Politics and Quantitative Methods. My research interests include representation, public opinion, institutions, and local politics. Specifically, I am interested in investigating public opinion on municipal policies and the effects of descriptive representation on municipal policy-making. Before coming to MIT, I graduated with a B.A. in Political Science from Brigham Young University.
